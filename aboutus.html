Our Mission

We present a currated database of the best capsites in the vast woods and backcountry of the World Wide Web wilderness.We increase access to advanture for the public while promoting a safe and respectful use of resources.The expert wilderness trekkers on our staff persoanlly verify each campsite to make sure that they are up to our standards.We also present a platform for campers to share reviews on campsites they have visited with each other.
